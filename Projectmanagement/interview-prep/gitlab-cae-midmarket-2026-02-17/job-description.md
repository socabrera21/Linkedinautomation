Primary point of contact for prospective and existing customers in the mid-market segment. Help organizations modernize how they plan, build, secure, and ship software with GitLab's AI-powered DevSecOps platform. Own a broad book of business, serve as strategic trusted advisor, prospect, run complex sales cycles, and guide customers to achieve business outcomes while supporting adoption and reducing churn.

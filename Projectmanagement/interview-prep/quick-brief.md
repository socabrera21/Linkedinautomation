## Fortreum in 3 Sentences

Fortreum is a Top 5 FedRAMP 3PAO and newly authorized CMMC C3PAO that helps cloud companies and defense contractors pass complex security audits so they can sell to the federal government. In January 2026, Gryphon Investors (a $10B+ AUM PE firm) completed a majority growth recapitalization -- the biggest inflection point in the company's history. ## XRAMP in 2 Sentences

XRAMP is Fortreum's continuous assessment platform that consolidates 6-11 compliance frameworks (FedRAMP, SOC 2, ISO, CMMC, PCI) into one normalized, year-round workstream -- replacing the chaos of annual audit scrambles. The pitch: "assess once, reuse many" -- evidence submitted for one framework satisfies overlapping requirements across all of them.

# STAR Method Stories Template

## How to Use STAR

**S**ituation - Set the context (1-2 sentences)
**T**ask - Describe your responsibility (1 sentence)
**A**ction - Explain what YOU did (3-4 specific actions)
**R**esult - Share quantifiable outcomes (metrics, learnings)

## Practice Delivery

### Timing Guidelines
- **Full STAR story:** 90-120 seconds
- **Situation + Task:** 20-30 seconds (setup)
- **Action:** 45-60 seconds (most detail here)
- **Result:** 20-30 seconds (impact)

### Delivery Tips
1. **Be specific** - Use real numbers, names, timelines
2. **Focus on YOUR actions** - Say "I" not "we" when describing your role
3. **Show, don't tell** - Instead of "I'm persistent," tell a story showing persistence
4. **Connect to role** - Always relate back to SparkPlug requirements
5. **Practice out loud** - Record yourself, time it, refine

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
❌ Rambling situation setup (keep it brief)
❌ Vague actions ("I worked hard")
❌ No measurable results
❌ Taking credit for team wins without acknowledging others
❌ Negative language about previous employers
❌ Stories that end in failure without learning

## Methodology & Process Questions

### "Are you familiar with MEDDPICC?"
**Your answer:**

"Absolutely - it's been my primary qualification framework at Agiloft and I've used variants of it throughout my career. I use it to qualify every opportunity: identifying the Economic Buyer early, understanding Decision Criteria and the Decision Process, mapping the Paper Process so procurement and security reviews don't surprise me late in the cycle, building a Champion who can sell internally when I'm not in the room, and always knowing who the Competition is.

The metric I'm most disciplined about is identifying pain. If I can't articulate the customer's pain in their words - not my marketing language - I know I don't have a real opportunity yet."

**Confidence: 5/5**

---

### "Tell me about your prospecting approach"
**Your answer:**

"I take a multi-threaded, intent-driven approach. At Agiloft, I built a $2M+ pipeline by combining intent data from tools like 6Sense and ZoomInfo with targeted multi-persona outreach. Instead of single-threading to one buyer, I map the full buying committee upfront and run coordinated sequences hitting three to four stakeholders simultaneously with persona-specific messaging.

For example, in a typical CLM deal, Legal gets pain-focused messaging around contract bottlenecks, IT gets integration and automation angles, and Security gets compliance messaging. This approach not only generates more pipeline - it generates better-qualified pipeline because I'm building multi-stakeholder engagement from day one."

**Confidence: 5/5**

---

### "What's your experience with mid-market sales specifically?"
**Your answer:**

"Mid-market is where I've spent the majority of my career. At Jamf, I managed a mid-enterprise territory for three and a half years - accounts in that 500 to 2,000 employee range. At Rapid7, I was specifically a Mid-Market AE working $400M+ revenue companies.

## Quick Reminders for Recruiter Screens

1. **Keep answers to 90 seconds max** - recruiters are screening for fit, not depth
2. **Use numbers** - quota attainment %, deal sizes, pipeline generated
3. **Show you've done research** - mention Duo Agent Platform, CREDIT values, Gartner recognition
4. **Be enthusiastic but not desperate** - you're evaluating them too
5. **Ask about next steps** - "What does the rest of the process look like?"
6. **The timezone question is coming** - have your answer ready

## Company Overview

### Mission & Vision
Crescendo.ai developed the world's first **augmented-AI customer experience (CX) platform** that combines the power of AI with human domain expertise to deliver outcome-driven results for fast-moving, digitally-native enterprises.

### Core Value Proposition
- **AI + Superhumans**: Working together to resolve what bots can't and BPOs won't
- **Pay-for-Outcomes Model**: Total Outcome Guarantee for quality, speed, and customer satisfaction
- **Fully Automated Tier 1**: Instant, multilingual support available 24/7

### Products & Services
**The Crescendo Platform:**
- Directs foundational AI models to conduct sales or customer support conversations within the client domain
- Blends artificial intelligence with human expertise for superior customer experience
- First AI-native contact center platform
- Provides both technology platform AND outsourced agents

### Market Position
- **Valuation**: $500 million (as of October 2024)
- **Total Funding**: $50 million in total financing
- **Lead Investor**: General Catalyst (Series C)
- **ARR Target**: Exceeding $100M ARR (announced September 2025)
- **Global Reach**: Operations across 6 continents
- **Customer Base**: 200+ customers (post-PartnerHero acquisition)

### Total Addressable Market
Disrupting the **$741 billion global contact center market**

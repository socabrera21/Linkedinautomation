# Company Intelligence: GitLab

## Company Overview
- **Founded:** 2011 (Dmitriy Zaporozhets & Sid Sijbrandij)
- **HQ:** San Francisco (all-remote, no physical office)
- **Employees:** ~2,375
- **Public:** NASDAQ: GTLB (IPO October 2021)
- **Revenue:** ~$947M projected FY2026 (25% YoY growth)
- **Customers:** 10,000+ paying; 1,229 at $100K+ ARR; 123 at $1M+ ARR

## CEO Transition (Critical Context)
- Sid Sijbrandij stepped down December 2024 (cancer diagnosis) to Executive Chairman
- **Bill Staples** named CEO - former CEO of New Relic, prior roles at Microsoft/Adobe
- Signals focus on scaling commercial execution and enterprise GTM maturity

## Market Position
- **Primary market:** AI-powered DevSecOps Platform (full SDLC in one app)
- **Core pitch:** Eliminate toolchain sprawl - replace 8-12 point solutions with one platform
- **Gartner 2025:** Leader in Magic Quadrant for DevOps Platforms (3rd consecutive year)
- **Gartner 2025:** Leader in Magic Quadrant for AI Code Assistants
- **Fortune 100:** Powers 50%+ of the Fortune 100

## Key Products
- **GitLab Premium ($29/user/mo):** CI/CD, planning, now includes Duo AI natively (GitLab 18)
- **GitLab Ultimate ($99/user/mo):** Full security scanning, compliance, portfolio planning
- **Duo Enterprise:** AI across full SDLC (vulnerability resolution, CI/CD troubleshooting, MR summarization)
- **Duo Agent Platform (GA January 15, 2026):** Agentic AI - Planner Agent, Security Analyst Agent, MCP client

## Competitors
| Competitor | Threat | Counter |
|---|---|---|

| Atlassian | Medium-High | Fragmented products vs. unified platform |
